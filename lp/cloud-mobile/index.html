Questions

Frequently asked

What's included in the $35/user/month price?
Each user gets an Australian virtual mobile number, unlimited local calls, SMS & MMS, call recording, voicemail, IVR access and reporting — all included in the one flat rate of $35 AUD per user per month. There are no per-minute charges, no add-on fees, and no lock-in contracts.
How long does it take to get set up?
Most teams are live the same day. After you sign up, our Sydney team provisions your numbers, configures your IVR and routing, and helps your team download the app. New numbers are issued immediately. If you're porting existing numbers, that process takes 3–5 business days.
Can I keep my existing business phone numbers?
Yes. Jet can port your existing local numbers (02, 03, 07), 1300 and 1800 numbers. Just provide the details during onboarding and our team will manage the port for you.
Where is my call data stored?
All call recordings, contact data, and account information are stored on Australian infrastructure and never leave Australia. This is important for businesses with privacy and compliance requirements under Australian law.
What happens if I want to cancel?
You can cancel at any time — just let us know before your next billing date and you won't be charged. There are no cancellation fees, no minimum term penalties, and no complicated exit processes. We're month-to-month because we want to earn your business every month.
Does Jet work on mobile phones and computers?
Yes. Jet runs on iOS, Android, and desktop (Mac and Windows). Your team uses the app on whatever device they already carry — no new hardware required.
