Phone System Management

Australian Mobile Number Geography

 

In Australia, geographic routing for mobile phone numbers relies on a network of mobile towers known as MOLIs. These MOLIs play a crucial role in determining the routing of calls by identifying the location of the mobile device. When a call is made from a mobile phone, the system can route the call to the nearest appropriate destination based on the specific MOLI that the call signal passes through. This method ensures efficient and accurate call routing by leveraging the physical location of the mobile device as indicated by the MOLI tower it is connected to.
