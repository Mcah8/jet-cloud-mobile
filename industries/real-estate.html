Agents leave. Their contacts shouldn't go with them.

Real estate is a relationship business. When agents use personal mobile numbers for client calls, the agency has no visibility into those conversations — and no way to retain those relationships if the agent moves on. Jet Cloud Mobile keeps business communications in the business, where they belong, without slowing your agents down or asking them to carry a second phone.

How it helps

How Jet Cloud Mobile works for real estate

Retain client numbers when agents leave

Business numbers belong to the agency, not the agent. When a team member moves on, their number stays with the business. Calls are immediately diverted to another agent from the Jet portal — buyers and vendors never reach a disconnected number.

A professional business number on any smartphone

Every agent gets their own dedicated business mobile number through the Jet app on their existing device. Buyers and vendors always see a business number. Personal calls and business calls stay completely separate — no mixing, no confusion.

Take calls from anywhere

Jet Cloud Mobile works over Wi-Fi or mobile data. Agents can take and make calls from a property, on the road, or working from home — all under the same business number, with no disruption to the client experience.

Full visibility for principal and management

See all calls and SMS across your agents in the Jet Hub portal. Track activity, review call history, and identify high-performing agents or missed opportunities — all without asking your team to change the way they work.

Call recording

Record calls for training, dispute resolution, and record keeping. AI transcription makes it easy to search and review conversations without listening to every recording. Available for $10/month per number.

Remote management from the portal

Update call routing and voicemail for any agent from the Jet portal — without touching their device. Ideal for principals managing multiple agents or offices.
