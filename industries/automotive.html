Leads go cold when they can't reach your team. Or worse — they follow the salesperson out the door.

In automotive sales, speed and accessibility are everything. When sales staff use personal mobile numbers, the dealership loses visibility over enquiries, has no way to track lead follow-up, and risks losing customer relationships when a staff member leaves. How it helps

How Jet Cloud Mobile works for automotive

Never miss a lead

Every call to your sales team is logged in the Jet Hub portal. See missed calls, call duration, and follow-up activity across your entire floor. Managers can identify gaps and act before a lead goes cold.

Dedicated business number for every salesperson

Each team member gets their own business mobile number through the Jet app on their existing smartphone. Customers always reach a professional business number — not a personal mobile. Sales calls and personal calls stay completely separate.

Retain customer relationships when staff turn over

Automotive dealerships experience high staff turnover. When a salesperson leaves, their business number stays with the dealership. Calls are immediately diverted to another team member — customers stay in contact and no relationship is lost.

Call recording for training and quality

Record sales calls to coach new staff, review objection handling, and maintain quality standards. AI transcription makes it easy to search recordings by keyword or topic without listening to every call. Available for $10/month per number.

CRM integration — log every call automatically

Calls are automatically logged against the relevant lead or customer record in your CRM. No manual entry, no missed follow-up notes. Your sales team focuses on selling, not administration.

Visibility across the dealership

Sales managers and dealer principals get a clear view of all communication activity across the team — calls, SMS, and recordings — in one place. No more chasing updates or relying on reps to self-report.

Works on the floor, on the road, and from home

Jet Cloud Mobile runs over Wi-Fi or mobile data. Sales reps can take enquiry calls from the showroom floor, on a test drive, or while working remotely. The same business number works everywhere.

Service team support

Extend Jet Cloud Mobile to your service and parts teams for the same visibility and professionalism. Service advisers can call and text customers from a dedicated business number, keeping all communication traceable.
