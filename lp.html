Questions

Common questions

Do my staff need a new phone?

No. Your staff install the Jet app on their existing iPhone or Android. Their business number and personal number are completely separate — one phone, two numbers.

What happens when a staff member leaves?

Their business number stays with your company. From the Jet Hub portal, you can instantly divert their number to another team member — customers never reach a disconnected number.

Is there a contract or lock-in period?

No contracts. You can scale up or down and cancel at any time. Add users as your team grows — you only pay for what you use.

Can I keep my existing number?

Yes. You can port your existing mobile or landline number to Jet Cloud Mobile, or get a new dedicated business number — whichever works best for your team.

How does call recording work?

Call recording is available as an add-on for $10/month per number. Recordings are stored securely in the Jet Hub portal and converted to searchable text using AI transcription.

What CRMs does it integrate with?

Jet Cloud Mobile integrates with leading CRM platforms to automatically log calls against the right contact. Contact our team to confirm compatibility with your specific CRM.
