Questions

Frequently asked

Can I keep my existing number when switching from Aircall?
Yes. Jet can port your existing local (02, 03, 07) and 1300/1800 numbers. New 04XX mobile numbers are issued as part of onboarding. The porting process typically takes 3–5 business days.
Is Jet Cloud Mobile suitable for a team using Aircall for call centre queues?
Jet includes IVR, call queues, call routing, and ring groups — so yes, teams currently using Aircall's queuing features will find comparable functionality in Jet Hub.
How does Jet's pricing compare once you add Aircall's call charges?
Aircall's base plan starts around $30 USD (~$46 AUD) per user/month but adds per-minute charges for calls. Jet starts at $35 AUD and includes unlimited local calls. For most Australian teams making regular calls, Jet works out significantly cheaper in practice.
Does Jet integrate with CRM platforms like Salesforce and HubSpot?
Yes. Jet integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, and other major CRMs via our call tracking and webhook capabilities. Speak to our team about your specific integration requirements.
How long does migration from Aircall take?
Most teams are live on Jet within 1–2 business days. New 04XX numbers are provisioned same day. Our onboarding team handles the configuration of your callflows, IVR and user setup, so there's minimal disruption to your operations.
