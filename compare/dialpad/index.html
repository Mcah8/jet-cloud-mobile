Questions

Frequently asked

Does Jet have AI calling features like Dialpad?
Jet focuses on the core calling features Australian businesses need: virtual mobile numbers, recording, SMS, IVR and reporting. We don't bundle AI transcription into our plans. If AI call summaries are a core requirement, Dialpad may suit you — but for most businesses, the tradeoff isn't worth the cost or complexity.
How does Jet's price compare to Dialpad for a team of 10?
At 10 users, Jet costs $350 AUD/month fixed. Dialpad's standard plan starts around $37 AUD equivalent, but that climbs once you add recording, international calls, or AI features. For most Australian teams, Jet comes out equal or cheaper when you factor in USD conversion and add-ons.
Can I migrate my Dialpad numbers to Jet?
Local (02, 03, 07) and 1300/1800 numbers can be ported to Jet. VoIP-only numbers from Dialpad may not be portable, but our team will assess your specific situation. New 04XX mobile numbers are issued on the same day you sign up.
Does Jet integrate with Salesforce and other CRMs?
Yes. Jet supports integration with Salesforce, HubSpot, and other CRM platforms via our call tracking and webhook capabilities. Speak to our team about your specific stack.
How quickly can we go live after leaving Dialpad?
Most teams are live within 1 business day. New numbers are provisioned same-day, and our onboarding team configures your IVR, callflows and users so your team experiences minimal downtime during the switch.
