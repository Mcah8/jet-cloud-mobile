FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are there any setup fees?

No. There are no setup fees on any plan. You pay only your monthly plan cost plus any add-ons you choose.

Can I change plans?

Yes. You can switch between Pay Per Call and Call Bundled at any time from the Jet portal.

What happens if I go over my included calls?

On the Call Bundled plan, excess calls to landlines and mobiles are charged at 6c/min. Excess SMS at 10c each.

Is there a minimum contract?

No. Jet Cloud Mobile is month to month with no lock-in contract. Cancel or scale at any time.

Can I port my existing number?

Yes. Number porting is free. A $50 port-out fee applies if you later move your number to a different provider.
