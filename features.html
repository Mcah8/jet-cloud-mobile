No second handset

Business and personal numbers on one device. Staff use the Jet app on the phone they already carry — no hardware to buy, configure, or manage.

Work from anywhere

Calls and SMS work over Wi-Fi or mobile data from any location. Your team can work from home, interstate, or overseas without missing a call.

Multi-device

One number, multiple devices. Use your business number on your phone, tablet, and desktop simultaneously — calls ring across all of them.

Call recording

Record calls automatically for training, compliance, and quality assurance. Recordings are stored securely in the cloud and accessible any time via the Jet Portal.

Reporting & visibility

See all call and SMS activity across your team in real time. Export reports for analysis, auditing, or compliance from the Jet Portal.

CRM integrations

Connect with Salesforce and other CRMs. Calls are logged automatically and you can click-to-dial directly from your CRM — no manual data entry.

Retain numbers when staff leave

Business numbers belong to the company, not the employee. When someone leaves, the number stays — no disruption to customers or colleagues.

Remote control via Jet Portal

Manage all users, numbers, call routing, and settings from one central admin dashboard. Add a new staff member in minutes — no IT team required.

Call flow builder

Set up IVR menus, call queues, business hours routing, and voicemail — all from the Jet Portal. No technical knowledge required.

SMS & MMS

Send and receive SMS and MMS from your business number. Conversations are logged alongside your call history in the Jet Portal.

Business hours & after-hours

Set the hours your number rings. After hours, calls go to voicemail or a custom message automatically — so you can switch off without missing anything.

Number porting

Already have a number you want to keep? Port it to Jet Cloud Mobile for free. The process is handled by the Jet team — no downtime.
